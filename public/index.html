Connect, Date, and Socialize

Pure Match is on a mission to bring like-minded followers of Christ together in a safe, innovative, and FREE networking app! Join us as we transform singlehood into the excitingm enriching FUN season it was meant to be.

Connect as a community

Dating and marriage may not be the sole focus of discipleship, but we all need connection. As Christians, it's important to have deep relationships with like-minded believers we trust and respect; our app facilities that.

Form organic relationships

Pure Match at its heart is a place for all disciples (single and married) to connect, network, and Socialize in order to strengthen the bond of kingdom of Heaven on earth.

Make meaningful conversations

Pure Match hopes to foster and grow relationships between like-minded disciples in a healthy community. Connect with Christians you have things in common with. Our enviroment fosters many lasting partnerships such as network conversations and even marriage.
